xxivips /// Audiovisual Works & Projection Mapping

As a specialist in projection mapping for audiovisual creations, I work with the artists to visually enhance their concepts and ideas.

I took part in several immersive shows like Van Gogh (Cathédrale d'Images), Couleurs Cézanne, Venise and Picasso (Gianfranco Iannuzzi) or even Australia, (Jean Charbonneau).

I work internationally for companies around the globe like Grande Exhibition (Melbourne, Australia), Art Science Museum (Singapore), Corporate Theatre Productions (Perth, Australia), culturespaces (Baux de Provence, France)<80> setting up audiovisual artworks and installations as well as corporate events.
